Our Team at Leyao Wang Lab

In the Leyao Wang lab, we integrated microbiology, immunology, and molecular biology with public health to understand how host-microbe interactions impact human health.

Our lab is part of the Institute of Human Immunology at the Shenzhen Medical Academy of Research and Translation (SMART) and is affiliated with the Microbiome Science program of SMART. Graduate students are typically recruited through the SMART PhD program. Dr. Leyao Wang is a Principal Investigator at SMART.
