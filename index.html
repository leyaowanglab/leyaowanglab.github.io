Our lab focuses on the human microbiome and diseases: Early Life Microbiome

Our Team at Leyao Wang Lab

In the Leyao Wang lab, we integrated microbiology, immunology, and molecular biology with public health to understand how host-microbe interactions impact human health.

Our lab is part of the Institute of Human Immunology at the Shenzhen Medical Academy of Research and Translation (SMART), Shenzhen, China. Dr. Leyao Wang is a Principal Investigator at SMART.
