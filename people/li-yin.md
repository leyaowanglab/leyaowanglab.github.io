### Introduction

Li Yin is a PhD student from the Westlake-SMART PhD Program in 2025, She has chosen to conduct her second rotation in Leyao Lab. During her master's degree, she has mainly researched on the vaccines and antibodies for African Swine Fever Virus. In Leyao Lab, she will perform the microbiome-host immune interaction research through the microbe-epithelia-monocyte co-culture model.
